Cyber.Bet Cup is a CS:GO and Dota 2 tournament series held by one of the leading esports bookmaker Cyber.Bet. Such online tournaments are the backbone of the whole esports as they let new players to compete versus experienced pros and so, get experience and possibility to earn prize money. Those events were the staging ground for s1mple, ZywOo, Miracle, JerAx and other Dota 2 and CS:GO superstars.

The first Cyber.Bet CS:GO cup was played on April 6-12 in 2020. Eight teams from CIS and Europe featuring NAVI Junior, Heretics, Movistar Riders and others competed for the prize pool of $10 000. The viewers could follow such players as two times Major winner Fabien “kioShiMa” Fiey, legendary Major champion Mikhail “Dosia” Stolyarov aka “X-GOD”, Brazilian Major finalist Lucas “steel” Lopes as well as many young prospects.Team Heretics featuring kioShiMa became the tournament winner.
